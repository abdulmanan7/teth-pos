# Unit of Measurement - Quick Reference

## 10 Standard Units

| Unit | Type | Use Case | Example |
|------|------|----------|---------|
| **piece** | Countable | Individual items | T-shirts, books, toys |
| **box** | Countable | Boxed items | Cereal boxes, shoe boxes |
| **pack** | Countable | Packed items | Card packs, snack packs |
| **dozen** | Countable | 12 units | Eggs, donuts |
| **kg** | Weight | Heavy items | Coffee, flour, sugar |
| **gram** | Weight | Light items | Spices, salt, powder |
| **liter** | Liquid | Large volumes | Milk, oil, juice |
| **ml** | Liquid | Small volumes | Perfume, medicine |
| **meter** | Length | Long items | Fabric, rope, cable |
| **cm** | Length | Short items | Ribbon, tape |

## Custom Units

| Unit | Use Case | Example |
|------|----------|---------|
| **bottle** | Beverages | Wine, beer, water |
| **jar** | Preserved items | Jam, honey, pickles |
| **roll** | Paper products | Toilet paper, tape |
| **bundle** | Grouped items | Firewood, sticks |
| **carton** | Packaged items | Milk carton, juice carton |
| **crate** | Large containers | Fruit crate, vegetable crate |
| **pallet** | Bulk storage | Wholesale quantities |
| **tube** | Pharmacy items | Cream, toothpaste |
| **can** | Canned items | Beans, soup, soda |
| **bag** | Bagged items | Chips, flour, rice |
